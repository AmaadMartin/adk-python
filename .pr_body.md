**Problem**: Workflows currently crash or lose progress when resuming from interruptions across turns. Rehydrating long-running workflows with nested interrupts relies on `DynamicNodeScheduler`, which does not properly rebuild node status, inputs, and interrupts from event history upon turn resumption.

**Solution**: Implemented `_process_resume` to extract the most recent checkpoint (`agent_state["nodes"]`) across session history and reconstruct `NodeState`, re-applying `resume_inputs`, resolving matching `interrupts`, and resetting unblocked nodes to `PENDING`. This guarantees that interrupted sub-actions continue smoothly across user turns.
